Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Quiz Deck

How do I create my first quiz?

Creating your first quiz is simple! After logging in, click on the 'Create Quiz' button on your dashboard. You can then add questions, set answers, and customize your quiz settings. Once you're satisfied, click 'Publish' to make your quiz available.

Can I embed quizzes on my website?

Yes! Quiz Deck provides an embed code for each quiz you create. Simply copy the code and paste it into your website's HTML. The quiz will appear seamlessly integrated with your site's design.

How are the pricing tiers calculated?

Our pricing is based on the number of quizzes you can create and the number of quiz attempts allowed per month. The Basic plan includes up to 5 quizzes and 100 attempts, Pro offers unlimited quizzes with 1,000 attempts, and Enterprise provides unlimited quizzes and attempts.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all Pro features during the trial period.

How secure is my quiz data?

We take data security very seriously. All quiz data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and regularly perform security audits to ensure your data remains protected.
